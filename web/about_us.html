About Us

Quizza is a real-time multiplayer quiz game created by Vakaris Perliba and Vitas Novickas. Our goal is to provide an engaging and educational experience for players of all ages.

We are two students passionate about technology and education, and we built Quizza to combine these interests into a fun and interactive platform.

Our team is committed to maintaining user privacy and ensuring a safe gaming environment. We do not collect personal data beyond the display name you enter, and no account or sign-up is required.
